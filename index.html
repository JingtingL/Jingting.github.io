Stop AAPI hate


Hello, welcome to our website!

This website was made to bring awareness to AAPI hate and information on how to support the AAPI community.

During the Covid-19 pandemic, Asian hate has risen, with people using the fact that the virus originated from an Asian country to excuse their hatred.

It is happening all over the world unfortunately, but it is increasing Asian visability and the struggles the community faces.

In this website, you may explore the history of Asian discrimination in America, current events concerning Asian hate, and resources and information on how to help.

We hope that you will speak up on Asian hate and discrimination!
