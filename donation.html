Help out by donating to Asian organizations:


  • In some areas where Asian hate crimes has been severe, volunteer groups have stepped up to look out for these attacks. Visit your city website to see if they may be offering a program like this.
  • People have volunteered to pass out whistles to people to keep them safe; see if your city offers this.
  • Partcipate in civic activities and write to your elected officials if there is change you want to see in your community.
  • Advocate or protest for protections of Asians and Pacific Islanders.



  • Donate to organizations that help the AAPI community and support Asian and Pacific Islander businesses.
